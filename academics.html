Our Academic Programs

We offer a comprehensive curriculum designed to develop critical thinking, creativity, and global competency.

Primary School (Grades 1-5)

Foundation in core academics with emphasis on language development, mathematics, sciences, and arts. Interactive learning environment fostering curiosity and creativity.

Middle School (Grades 6-8)

Interdisciplinary approach combining core subjects with technology and life skills. Development of research skills and independent thinking.

High School (Grades 9-12)

International Baccalaureate (IB) Program and Advanced Placement (AP) courses. University preparatory curriculum with specialized tracks in STEM, Business, and Humanities.

Special Features

  • Small Class Sizes: Maximum 20 students per class for personalized attention
  • Experiential Learning: Field trips, projects, and real-world applications
  • Global Partnerships: Exchange programs with schools in 20+ countries
  • Advanced Facilities: Modern laboratories, technology centers, and sports complexes
  • Mentoring Programs: One-on-one guidance from experienced educators
  • Extracurricular Activities: Clubs, competitions, and leadership opportunities
