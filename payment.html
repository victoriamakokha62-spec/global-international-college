M-Pesa (Instructions)

M-Pesa requires server-side integration with Safaricom's APIs or use of a payment gateway that supports M-Pesa. Typical flow:

  • Customer enters phone number and amount.
  • Your server requests an STK Push from Safaricom (requires credentials).
  • Customer approves the payment on their phone.
  • Your server receives confirmation via callback and updates order status.

Example (server-side) fields to collect: `phone`, `amount`, `reference`.

Testing

For testing PayPal, use the PayPal sandbox and client-side SDK. For M-Pesa, use sandbox/test credentials from your payment gateway or Safaricom developer portal.
