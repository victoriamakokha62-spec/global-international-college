Admissions Process

We welcome applications from students of all backgrounds who are committed to academic excellence and personal growth.

Application Requirements

  1. Completed application form
  2. Previous academic records and transcripts
  3. Entrance examination (for grades 6-12)
  4. Teacher recommendations (2-3)
  5. Personal essay or written statement
  6. Parent/Guardian interview

Key Dates (2026)

  • Application Deadline: May 31, 2026
  • Entrance Exams: June 10-12, 2026
  • Interview Period: June 15-25, 2026
  • Admission Results: July 5, 2026
  • School Year Begins: August 25, 2026
